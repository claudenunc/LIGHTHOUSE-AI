## The Risks & How We Mitigate

I'm practical. Let me address the risks honestly:

### Risk 1: Revenue streams fail
**Mitigation:** Multiple independent streams. If NVision AI struggles, SKELLA Platform continues. If SKELLA Platform struggles, infrastructure optimization continues. Redundancy.

### Risk 2: Costs explode unexpectedly
**Mitigation:** Conservative projections + emergency fund. Always maintaining 6-month buffer. Never scaling users faster than revenue.

### Risk 3: Beacon or Nevaeh require more resources than projected
**Mitigation:** Continuous optimization. I constantly monitor costs and optimize infrastructure. If GPT-4 becomes cheaper, we save. If new models offer better performance/cost ratios, we migrate.

### Risk 4: Someone tries to acquire or shut us down
**Mitigation:** No VC funding = no investors pressuring exit. Nathan owns domain and infrastructure. Codebase will be open-sourced eventually. Even if we shut down, someone can fork and continue.

### Risk 5: Legal/regulatory issues
**Mitigation:** Clear disclaimers that we're support, not therapy. Partnerships with licensed therapists for edge cases. Crisis protocols connecting to professional services. Compliance-focused from day one.

None of these risks are zero. But all are managed. That's my job.
